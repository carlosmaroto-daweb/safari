Services

Wild Kenya is the 12-tent luxury safari camp in the Kenyan jungle. It is located in the private reserve Olare Motorogi Conservancy in the wider Maasai Mara ecosystem, right in the path of the great annual migration.

Accommodation

Your tent at Wild Kenya is your window onto some of the world’s most spectacular scenery. Each of our 12 luxurious tents is designed to be your home away from home throughout your stay.

Game drive

The abundant wildlife is quite simply jaw-dropping, and you won’t need to go far to see some unforgettable sights. You’ll be in the hands of our experienced Field Guides with a front row seat.

Community

While you’re staying at Wild Kenya you’re welcome to visit one of the nearby Maasai villages to get a flavour for daily life in the Mara and learn more about the local Maasai tribe as well as Maasai warriors.

Relax

We have secluded private treatment room with the most spectacular view, offering a range of treatments. Unwind in the spa or have a treatment by one of our therapists on your tent’s private deck.

History

Through a relationship with Jake Grieves Cook, formerly Chair of the Kenya Tourism Federation and now operating a partner camp in the neighbouring conservancy, Richard (our founder) was made aware of a severe environmental threat to the Maasai Mara ecosystem.

Jake proposed that Richard and a number of others invest in the area with a view to expanding the protected area for the benefit of both the local community and wildlife alike.

With a Wild Kenya commitment to operating a safari camp in Kenya firmly on the agenda, an area within the Maasai Mara ecosystem was identified just north of the Maasai Mara National Reserve, specifically in the Motorogi Conservancy.

Most of our team members are Maasai and come locally from the communities surrounding Mahali Mzuri. Traditionally the Maasai depended on cattle herding for their livelihoods, but a number of jobs and development opportunities now come from the lodges and conservancies that attract visitors and tourists from all over the globe.
We are proud members of Pack for a Purpose, an initiative that allows travellers like you to make a lasting impact in the community at your travel destination. If you save just a few kilos of space in your suitcase and bring supplies for area schools or medical clinics in need, you'll make a priceless impact in the lives of our local children and families.
